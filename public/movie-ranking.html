The Best Movies According to Scott

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Scarface (1983)

Loved the Movie for its Action Packed Scenes on the Rise & Fall of a cuban refugee, who flees to america and becomes a top criminal Entrepreneur.

The Godfather (1972)

A captivating film that brings you into the world of the Italian American Mafia and there operations that cause business to interfere with family matters of loyalty and traitors.

Star Wars: Episode 1 - The Phantom Menace (1999)

My favorite film of my childhood. Was always astounded by the animations and martial arts that where used in this film aswell as the uniqueness of the fictional characters portrayed such as Jar Jar Binks and Darth Maul.
